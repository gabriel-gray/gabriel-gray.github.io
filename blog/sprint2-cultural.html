What is your long term goal and or career pathway?

I would love to do this while travelling and living in different countries. At some point maybe I would love to teach people about what I have learned in web development.

Human skills I would live to learn/develop at EDA

I would like to develop and work on my collaboration skills. Being able to work as a team is important in this industry, so I would love to see these skills develop, especially with doing the course online, it will be an important skill to have developed.

My Strengths and limitations

My main strengths are being extroverted and being dependable. As stated in the Cultural 1 blog, because of these strengths I enjoy working in team environments and tackling common goals while helping my peers.

My main limitation would be my self-confidence, I can sometimes second guess and have self-doubts about something and focus more on my feelings too much rather than thinking about my doubts critically.
