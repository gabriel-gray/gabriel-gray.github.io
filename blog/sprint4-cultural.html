My understanding on Neuroplasticity and Growth Mindset and how it influences my learning

Neuroplasticity is the brain's ability to change and grow overtime in response to it's enviroment. Changes can happen either fast or slow. A growth mindset is the belief that your are in con trol of your own ability and can learn an approve.

Thinking about having a growth mindset really helps give that extra layer I have presistence in my learning. Knowing it's the way I approach and tackle the task at hand and once I overcome the challenge, my knowledge of the subject expands. So, not only does it feel like I've completed a diffcult challenge, I feel like my knowledge on the subject has expanded.
